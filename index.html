What is Knitting?

Knitting is a method of ceating fabric by interlocking loops of yarn using knitting needles. It is one of the most popular forms of textile art that is used to ake garments,accessories, home decor etc.

Basic Tools:

  • Yarn: Comes in various weights(lace, worsted, bulky)and fibers(wool, cotton, acrylic etc).
  • Knitting Needles: Available in different materials (metal, bamboo, plastic) and styles:
  • Circular Needles:Good for knitting in the round or large projects.
  • Straight Needles: Good for flat pices eg.scarves.
  • Double-pointed Needles:Used for small circular knitting eg.socks.

Basic Techniques:

  1. Casting on:The foundation row of stiches.
  2. Knit Stitch: This is the most basic stitch, and creates a smooth fabric.
  3. Purl Stitch: Used with the knit stitch to create texture.
  4. Binding Off: Finshes the edge so that the stitches do not unravel.

Common Projects for beginners:

  • Scarves
  • Lttle decoration pieces
  • Beanies

Benefits of Knitting:

There are many benefits from knitting. Just to name a few, studdies have shown that knitting reduces stress and anxiety, enhances fine motor skills and offers creative expression.
